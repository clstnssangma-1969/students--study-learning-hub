62. What is the function of a modem?
To modulate digital signals from a computer into analog signals for transmission over telephone lines and to demodulate incoming analog signals back into digital signals.
76. Define topology in networking.
Topology refers to the physical or logical arrangement of computers and other devices (like printers, switches) in a network.
77. What is the difference between wired and wireless technology?
Wired technology uses physical cables (like Ethernet cables) to connect devices, while wireless technology uses radio waves or infrared signals to connect devices without physical cables.
78. What is the main drawback of linear bus topology?
The main drawback is that if the main cable (the backbone) fails or breaks, the entire network breaks down and becomes unusable.
